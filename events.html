Events Gallery


Tamanna Foundation (TF) in collaboration and association with honorable institutions and reputed individuals, conducted following events for the mutual benefit and upliftment of the spirits of the less privileged children of GOD. The directors of Tamanna Foundation, Mrs. Amita Jain and Mrs. Erina Jain look forward to organize many such exotic events in the interest of these special children thereby catering to the motto of Tamanna Foundation ~ “ Make them Smile “


March Pass & Dance Performance
Jai Vakeel School, 24th Feb, 2013

A March pass and Dance performance by students of Jai Vakeel School (Kalachowki) in Shree Suntar Youth group Cricket match. Students and Staff were provided with lunch, gifts, chocolates and return gifts.

Republic Day Celebration,
CJM school, 24th Jan 2013

Republic Day celebration at Christ Jesus Mary School (Clare road). Event was preceded with a speech and Quiz on our National leaders. We also distributed chocolates to them.

Workshop of Chocolate,
Kalbaug School, 6th Jul 2012

Organized workshop of Chocolate at Dr.Kalbaug School(Vile parle) for disabled children which taught them to make different type of chocolates which can be helpful to them in future.

Dental Camp, BIDM school,
6th & 7th Sep 2012

Conducted Dental Camp at Bombay Institution of Deaf and Mute School (Mazgaon). Further, Hearing Aid was also donated to 2 students.

Navratri Event, Jai Vakeel school,
8th Nov 2012

Sponsored gifts on Navratri event to the students of Jai Vakeel School (Kalachowki).

Disability Week Celebration,
CJM School, 6th Dec 2012

Celebrated Disability Week with students of Christ Jesus Mary School (Clare road) at at Phoenix Mall. Members of Rotaract Club of Bombay Hills South were invited to render themselves as volunteers. Children had a fun and play time in different rides accompanied with scrumptious snacks.

Christmas Party, BIDM School,
20th Dec 2012

Organized Christmas Party at Bombay Institution of Deaf and Mute School (Mazgaon) wherein students performed dance shows and were provided with gifts and snacks.

MIRAGE,
18th Sep 2011

“Mirage”, an initiative of Tamanna Foundation was organized at Birla Mathushri Hall to showcase the talent of the mentally and physically challenged children. These children got an opportunity to walk on the ramp and perform dance along with professional models. There were many guests invited, whose presence rejuvenated the spirit of the children and assured to stay in touch with the NGO for future assistance. Some of the well known guest at the Mirage were ‘Samay’ and ‘Bhavya’, commonly known as Gogi and Tapu who were more than glad to pose the shutterbugs with the children. The event promised to be a complete package to the audience.

Art & Craft Workshop,
BIDM School, 1-5th Nov 2011

Conducted a 5 day workshop in the form of “VOCATIONAL TRAINING”, at Bombay Institution for Deaf and Mutes School. The activities of 5 days spanned across learning new things; right from Handicrafts, Massage, Drawing , Paper bags , Frames to Bread crafts and many more. The workshop was forth led by many trained teachers. What better could bring a smile on the face of these children when they were rewarded with exciting gifts.

Diwali Party,BIDM School,
23rd Oct 2011

Organized a Diwali Party at Bombay Institution of Deaf and Mute School (Mazgaon) tossed with welcome song, dance and offcourse prayers to grace the occasion and exhibit our culture. The students showcased their potentials and abilities by competing in Rangoli competition, Diwali Lamp making competition and Diya competition. The competitions were sponsored by Tamanna Foundation.

Interschool Drawing Competition,
BIDM School, 31st Jul 2010

Organized Interschool Drawing competition (Theme based) at Bombay Institution of Deaf and Mute School (Mazgaon) which was witnessed by 27 schools around the city. There were 80 participants from various age categories who participated in drawing, painting, collage, and other activities. The chief guest of the event was Mr. Bhavya Gandhi (alias Tappu). The winners were awarded trophies and nonetheless the participants with consolation prizes.

Dance Competition, BIDM School,
15th Sept 2010

Organized Fancy Dress cum Dance competition at Bombay Institution of Deaf and Mute School (Mazgaon). We also donated Hearing Aid to two students
