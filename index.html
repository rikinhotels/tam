About

WHO WE ARE

We are TAMANNA FOUNDATION (TF), the pioneering, national and non-profit organization, established on 30th June, 2010. The TF has been fully dedicated to the cause of improving the lives of the differently abled children and has also achieved immense success in their venture. TF focuses special attention on helping children. TF is an initiative, to educate the society about Children with Special Needs.

Mission

OUR GOALS

TF has been formed with an objective of educating the society with regards to children with special needs. We would be conducting seminars, exhibitions & events for these children where they would not only be able to show case their skills but also allow the society to know them better. Our Aim is to prepare these young people for adult life, giving them confidence and skills to achieve their full potential.
